# Smart Contract Development for Community Childcare Cooperative\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces two comprehensive smart contracts that form the foundation of a decentralized community childcare cooperative platform. The implementation leverages Stacks blockchain technology with Clarity smart contracts to provide transparent, secure, and efficient management of cooperative childcare operations.\n\n## Smart Contracts Implemented\n\n### 1. Parent Coordination Contract (`parent-coordination.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Manages parent participation, duty assignments, and coordination logistics within the childcare cooperative.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Parent Registration**: Secure registration system for new cooperative members\n- **Duty Management**: Automated assignment and tracking of childcare responsibilities\n- **Availability Scheduling**: Weekly schedule management for parent availability\n- **Reliability Scoring**: Performance-based reliability tracking system\n- **Contribution Tracking**: Comprehensive monitoring of parent participation hours\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-parent`: Register new parents with validation checks\n- `assign-duty`: Administrative duty assignment with conflict prevention\n- `complete-duty`: Mark duties as completed with rating system\n- `set-weekly-schedule`: Personal availability and preference management\n- `update-reliability-score`: Administrative reliability score updates\n\n### 2. Child Development Tracking Contract (`child-development-tracking.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Comprehensive tracking system for child development milestones, educational progress, and health records.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Child Registration**: Secure child profile creation with parental authorization\n- **Milestone Recording**: Developmental achievement tracking with verification system\n- **Educational Activities**: Activity participation and progress monitoring\n- **Health Records**: Medical information and appointment management\n- **Progress Reports**: Comprehensive developmental progress documentation\n- **Caregiver Authorization**: Flexible permission system for authorized caregivers\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-child`: Child profile creation with medical and emergency information\n- `record-milestone`: Developmental milestone documentation with scoring\n- `add-educational-activity`: Educational program tracking and outcomes\n- `record-health-data`: Medical records and health monitoring\n- `create-progress-report`: Comprehensive developmental progress reporting\n- `authorize-caregiver`: Permission management for trusted caregivers\n\n## Technical Implementation Details\n\n### Security Features\n- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions ensuring only authorized users can access sensitive data\n- **Data
